Quick Setup Guide

  1. Add your admin panel domain and click "Set as Panel"
  2. Add your phishing domain(s) (purpose: campaign)
  3. Point DNS A records for all domains to this server
  4. Click "Verify DNS" to confirm resolution
  5. Click "Issue SSL" (Let's Encrypt) or provide cert paths (Manual)
  6. Restart the nginx proxy: docker restart nophish-nginx
